About Us

Shiboyan Inc. is a non profit organization that specializes in helping global charities and foundations strive towards success in their work and studies. Our goal is to provide support for those who are active in the world to help find a solution to the problems of humanity, whether it is world hunger, disease, or clean water and energy for everyone.
